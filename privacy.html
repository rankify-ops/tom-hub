Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

What This App Does

Tom's Hub is a personal operations dashboard for private use. It connects to third-party services (such as WHOOP) to display personal health and fitness data.

Data Collection

This app collects and stores data from connected services (WHOOP profile, recovery, sleep, strain, and workout data) solely for display within the dashboard. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

Data is stored in Vercel KV (Upstash Redis) and is accessible only through the dashboard. OAuth tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to the client.

Data Deletion

You can disconnect your WHOOP account at any time, which removes stored tokens and cached data.
